GenKin

With GenKin, you can create an immersive, interactive experience of your family tree, complete with your photos, videos, audio files, or text.

Create a family tree by entering the names of your family members. Upload media for each of your family members.

Need inspiration? Check out the gallery to see the trees your fellow users have created.
